Our Story

Rooted in Brooklyn, dedicated to your health.

Our History & Mission

At Lindenwood Rx Center (LiRx), we believe that pharmacy care should be personal, accessible, and deeply integrated into the community. Founded on the principles of trust and clinical excellence, our mission is to simplify healthcare for our patients while providing the highest standard of pharmaceutical care.

We are more than just a place to pick up medication. We are your healthcare partners, dedicated to offering comprehensive solutions—from modern digital tools to individualized clinical consultations.

What Makes LiRx Different?

Patient-First Approach

Every decision we make is centered around patient outcomes, not corporate bottom lines.

Modern Technology

We combine old-school personalized care with cutting-edge digital tracking, free delivery, and an intuitive dashboard.

Community Integration

We actively participate in local Brooklyn health fairs, school immunization drives, and senior care programs.

Our Journey

1998

The Beginning

Lindenwood Pharmacy first opened its doors as a small corner apothecary dedicated to serving the local neighborhood.

2010

Clinical Expansion

We expanded our footprint to include specialized compounding and a fully out-fitted immunization clinic.

2020

During the Pandemic

LiRx pivoted to offer free home delivery and safely fulfilled over 50,000 emergency prescriptions for the Brooklyn community.

2026

The Digital Era

Launching our new state-of-the-art digital ecosystem, offering patients unprecedented access to their healthcare via their devices.
